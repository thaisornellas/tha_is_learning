About me

My name is Thais, and I hold a Master’s degree in Physics with emphasis on Cosmology from the State University of Londrina (UEL). During my master’s, I worked as a programmer implementing concentration–mass relation models for the NumCosmo library, while also conducting data analysis and validation of cosmological codes. I was also a junior collaborator in the Dark Energy Science Collaboration (DESC), contributing to validation analyses of the Core Cosmology Library (CCL).

I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Physics from the State University of Maringá (UEM) in 2023, where I carried out research in photothermal techniques applied to the characterization of photosensitizing dyes for Photodynamic Therapy. In parallel, I worked as a Teaching Assistant, creating and solving didactic material in Applied Physics, which strengthened my skills in communication and organization.

I have strong interests in computational physics, algorithm development, and statistical data analysis, while also expanding my knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, and object-oriented programming logic. Beyond academia, I enjoy practicing and testing my skills in front-end web development, creating projects that also serve as a portfolio to showcase my abilities.

I am motivated by continuous learning, innovation, and generating a positive impact, values that connect with my personal purpose: to contribute to solutions that make a difference in people’s lives and help build a better future.
